Cherry Cheesecake

Description

This cherry cheesecake is a cross between a cherry cheesecake and a cherry cream cheese pie. It is delicious!

Ingrediends

  • 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • 1/3 cup lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 (9 inch) prepared graham cracker crust
  • 1 (21 ounce) can cherry pie filling

Steps

  1. Place condensed milk, softened cream cheese, lemon juice, and vanilla into a mixing bowl; beat until well blended.
  2. Pour mixture into pie crust. Chill in the refrigerator for 5 hours; do not freeze.
  3. Pour cherry pie filling on top of pie; serve.
